CUPE Local 2 Campaign

Fair TTC Service for Every Rider

Toronto riders deserve reliable, safe, and equitable transit in every neighbourhood—no matter their postal code or work schedule.

Get Campaign Updates

Why this matters

Uneven service hurts working families

Frequent delays and cutbacks hit shift workers, caregivers, and low-income riders the hardest, making daily travel stressful and expensive.

Longer waits reduce access

Inconsistent bus and streetcar frequencies can mean missed appointments, late arrivals, and fewer opportunities for those already facing barriers.

Reliable transit strengthens communities

Better TTC service supports local businesses, student mobility, and climate goals by making public transit a dependable first choice.

What we're asking for

  • Restore and protect frequent service on overcrowded routes.
  • Set minimum off-peak service standards across all wards.
  • Increase operator staffing to reduce cancellations and sudden gaps.
  • Publish transparent monthly service reliability reports for riders.
  • Commit to equitable transit planning with community input.
